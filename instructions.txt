- In order to execute the script in the Google Chrome Console follow the procedure:

  1 - Open a product page in Google Chrome
  2 - Right click -> Select ‘Inspect’
  3 - Select ‘Console’
  4 - Paste the script into console, execute it
  5 - The JSON Object will be logged at the end of execution.
